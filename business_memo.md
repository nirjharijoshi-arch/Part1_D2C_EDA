# Business Memo

## Key Findings

The overall customer churn rate is approximately 47%.

Customers with high recency values show dramatically higher churn rates, indicating inactivity is the strongest predictor of churn.

Silver-tier customers display higher churn rates than Gold and Platinum customers.

Customers with low recent web engagement and low purchase frequency also demonstrate elevated churn risk.

Support-ticket activity appears associated with churn and should be investigated further.

## Recommended Investigation

Before launching retention campaigns, the company should:

1. Prioritize inactive customers.
2. Improve engagement among Silver-tier members.
3. Investigate unresolved support issues.
4. Target customers with declining web activity.
5. Monitor purchase frequency changes as an early churn signal.
